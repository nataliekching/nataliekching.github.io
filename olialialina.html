My Boyfriend Came Back From the War
by Olia Lialina

Olia Lialina was born in Moscow in 1971 and now lives in Germany. She became an important voice in contemporary art and new media through her impact on recognizing the Internet as a medium for artistic expression and storytelling. She is most known for participating in the 1990s net.art scene. She's produced many network-based art works in the last two decades, including those named Agatha Appears, First Real Art Gallery, Online Newspapaer, and My Boyfriend Came Back from the War.

I thought that the art piece gave off an eerie feel in the way it used only black and white as well as silhouettes of men and objects. There wasn't a direct storyline, so I thought giving the audience a chance to interact with the piece was very unique so that eveyone can have an individualized experience with the art. When I began interacting with the piece, I was a little confused because I never interacted with something like this before but I found the experience suspenseful because I wasn't sure what would be after each click.
-Natalie
