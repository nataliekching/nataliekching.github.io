My Boyfriend came back from the War

1996- Olia Lialina


A project that Olia calls a "netfilm." The whole piece is a black and white, multi-linear story that processes through single clicks as frames split into subdivisions.
The main female talks about cheating, potential abuse, and there is some mention of marriage when their boyfriend returns home.
Each subdivion uncovered unravels more to the story.
Uses some animation and HTML to create an interactive, ominous story that builds with user engagement.
